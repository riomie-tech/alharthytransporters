How We Move The Impossible

What is considered oversized or OOG cargo?

expand_more

OOG means Out of Gauge. Any load exceeding 3.5m width, 4.2m height, or 20m length typically requires special permits. We handle transformers, turbine blades, vessels, and industrial machinery up to 300 tons.

We manage all GCC permits, route surveys, and police escorts in-house.

Which GCC countries do you operate in?

expand_more

UAE, KSA, and Oman. We have local offices and registered fleets in each country for faster permit approvals and customs clearance.

Cross-border moves include full documentation, convoy coordination, and border liaison support.

Do you provide crane hire and lifting services?

expand_more

Yes. We operate mobile cranes from 50T to 200T capacity, SPMT systems, and Goldhofer blade lifters. All operators are certified and equipment is ADR compliant.

We provide lifting plans, risk assessments, and site supervision as standard.

How long does permit approval take?

expand_more

Standard OOG permits: 3-7 working days. Complex routes with bridge calculations or night-time restrictions: 10-14 days.

We start the permit process immediately after contract award to avoid project delays.

Are you ADR certified for hazardous cargo?

expand_more

Yes. Our fleet and drivers hold full ADR certification for Class 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, and 9 dangerous goods. We provide UN packaging, placarding, and emergency response plans.

Safety documentation and MSDS review is completed before every ADR move.

Can I track my shipment in real time?

expand_more

All prime movers have GPS tracking with 5-minute updates. You receive a secure link for live location, ETA, and milestone alerts.

For critical projects, we provide daily photo updates and convoy lead contact details.
