Logistics & Operations

What regions do Al Harthy Transporters cover?

expand_more

We operate across the entire Middle East region, specializing in cross-border logistics between Oman, UAE, and Saudi Arabia. Our extensive network ensures seamless transitions at all major land ports with 48-hour mobilization to any major GCC industrial site.

Do you provide real-time tracking for shipments?

expand_more

Yes, all shipments are equipped with GPS tracking. Clients receive access to our portal for 24/7 real-time location updates, ETA calculations, and delivery confirmations. Live telematics included on all cold chain and hazardous cargo.

What is your maximum payload capacity?

expand_more

Our heavy low loader fleet handles up to 80 tons standard, with project-specific configurations reaching 120+ tons using hydraulic modular trailers. For oversized cargo, we engineer custom solutions with escort vehicles and route surveys.

Safety & Compliance

What are your safety protocols for hazardous cargo?

expand_more

All drivers handling hazardous materials undergo rigorous specialized training. Our fleet is equipped with state-of-the-art safety sensors and containment systems, fully compliant with international ADR standards and local GCC regulations.

Are your drivers certified for heavy-duty haulage?

expand_more

Every driver holds GCC heavy vehicle licenses plus specialized certifications for abnormal loads, ADR hazardous materials, and defensive driving. Continuous training is mandatory quarterly.

Who handles oversized permits and route surveys?

expand_more

We manage all permits end-to-end. Our team conducts route surveys, obtains Ministry of Transport approvals, coordinates police escorts, and handles customs clearance. Average permit lead time is 5-7 working days for Oman/UAE routes.

Billing & Payments

What payment terms do you offer for corporate clients?

expand_more

We offer Net 30 terms for approved corporate accounts. New clients may begin with 50% advance payment until credit is established. Wire transfer and approved corporate cards accepted.

Can I manage my invoices online?

expand_more

Yes, through our Client Portal, you can access your billing history, download current invoices, and set up automated payment reminders. Contact our finance department for portal access credentials.

What insurance coverage do you provide?

expand_more

All loads are covered by comprehensive cargo insurance up to $5M USD per shipment. We also carry public liability, environmental damage, and third-party insurance. Certificates of insurance provided with every quote.

Fleet Specifications

What types of trailers are available in your fleet?

expand_more

Our fleet includes 40ft flatbeds, extendable low-loaders up to 22m, tri-axle fuel tankers, water tankers, refrigerated units, and hydraulic modular trailers for loads exceeding 100 tons. See our Fleet page for full specs.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency transport?

expand_more

Yes. Our operations center runs 24/7 for critical shipments. Emergency mobilization within 6 hours for Oman/UAE, 12 hours for other GCC countries. Dedicated hotline available to contracted clients.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with your logistical needs and technical inquiries.
