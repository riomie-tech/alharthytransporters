Logistics & Operations

We operate across the entire Middle East region, specializing in cross-border logistics between Oman, UAE, and Saudi Arabia. Our extensive network ensures seamless transitions at all major land ports.

Safety & Compliance

All drivers handling hazardous materials undergo rigorous specialized training. Our fleet is equipped with state-of-the-art safety sensors and containment systems, fully compliant with international ADR standards and local GCC regulations.
Every driver holds GCC heavy vehicle licenses plus specialized certifications for abnormal loads, ADR hazardous materials, and defensive driving. Continuous training is mandatory quarterly.
