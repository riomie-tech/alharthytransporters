Dhofar Wind Farm Phase II

Sustainable Energy Infrastructure Logistics: Navigating complex terrains to power Oman's future.

CLIENT

Oman Power & Water Procurement Co.

LOCATION

Dhofar Region, Oman

DURATION

9 Months

UNITS

40 Wind Turbines

THE CHALLENGE

Oversized Cargo in Extreme Terrain

Transporting 75-meter turbine blades and 100-ton nacelles from Salalah Port to the mountain ridges of Dhofar presented unprecedented logistical hurdles.

The route required navigating narrow mountain passes with sharp switchbacks and constant exposure to high-wind corridors. Traditional haulage methods were insufficient for the extreme geometry of the mountain ascent.

THE SOLUTION

Precision Engineering & Specialized Equipment

Al Harthy deployed state-of-the-art Goldhofer blade lifters, allowing blades to be tilted up to 60 degrees.

  • check_circle
    Goldhofer Technology

    Utilizing hydraulic lifting systems to clear obstacles and mountain overhangs.

  • check_circle
    Multi-Axle Modularity

    Modular trailers customized for nacelle weight distribution over unstable ground.

  • check_circle
    Night-Time Operations

    Strictly controlled transport windows to ensure maximum safety and minimum public disruption.
