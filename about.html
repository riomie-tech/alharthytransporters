Our Story

25 Years of Moving Industry Forward

Al Harthy Transporters began with a single truck and a commitment to reliability. Today we operate 500+ specialized units across 12 strategic hubs in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and beyond.

We specialize in heavy-haul, abnormal loads, and ADR-certified hazardous cargo. Our clients include leading firms in mining, oil & gas, energy, and infrastructure.

What hasn’t changed is our promise: on-time delivery, zero-compromise safety, and transparent operations backed by real-time tracking.
