Our Story

20 Years of Moving Industry Forward

Al Harthy Transporters started with one truck and a promise: deliver every load with surgical precision, no matter the terrain.

Today we operate 50+ prime movers, 200T mobile cranes, and SPMT systems across UAE, KSA, and Oman. Our clients include ADNOC, Aramco, and major EPC contractors.

What hasn't changed is our promise: on-time delivery, zero-compromise safety, and treating every driver like family.
